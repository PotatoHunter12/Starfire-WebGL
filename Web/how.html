How to play

Here is a quick guide trough Starfire.

Use keys to move:
- to move to the forward, press ↑ or W
- to move to the backwards, press ↓ or S
- to move to the left, press ← or A
- to move to the right, press → or D
- to jump, press space bar

To hit the enemies, you can use right and left mouse click.

To heal with Starfire, stand on it for a few moments.
