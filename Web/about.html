About Starfire

Starfire is a game developed by three students at Faculty of Computer and Information Science, University of Ljubljana. This game was made for the course Computer Graphics in the year of 2023/2024.

The young and talented developers working on this game are Ambrož Perovšek, Lara Nićetin and Neja Kotnik. Working together they finalised this product in January after three and a half months of hard work.



Starfire lore

Starfire is a 3rd person 3D action fantasy RPG game, inspired by God of War, Apex, Totally accurate battlegrounds and Polytopia.
